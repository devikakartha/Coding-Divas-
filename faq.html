FAQs

Who can use this app? +

Anyone planning to travel alone by a public transport(buses or trains) can use this app.

Why use Travel Connect? +

If you are someone who hates travelling alone,Travel Connect is for you! It connects you with a travel companion for your whole journey.It also includes many other cool features.

Is Travel Connect free? +

Ofcourse,it is! Keeping you safe and happy is our priority.Travel Connect is completely free with absolutely no in-app experience.
