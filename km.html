"Travelling alone may not always be one's cup of tea.
Some may love to make friends or atleast have someone to look out for them on their journey.
And what if we face an unexpected crisis?
It could be something simple, from your hunger getting the better of you, to maybe even, god forbid, an accident!
This app will help solve all these problems and make your journey safer and smoother."
You can now connect with your co-travelers, and even the locals along the way.
You can also find local stores for your requirements.
In case you encounter a problem enroute, you can also contact your close friends or family,
the police, ambulance or even the fire force.
